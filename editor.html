In my previous studies I specialised in Spanish literature, specifically literature of the Spanish Golden Age (XVI and XVII centuries), providing the inspriation behind the Saber Caller literary e-journal.

I am currently studying for a second masters degree in Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge at the University of Bologna.
